# Hi, I'm Mateusz Szewczyk

I work at the intersection of **data analytics, business operations, and EV charging**. Based in Vienna, I use data to improve pricing, financial monitoring, partner operations, and decision-making for EV-charging roaming products.

I am currently pursuing an **MSc in Data Science at TU Wien**, where I am strengthening my foundations in machine learning, statistics, data engineering, and scalable analytics.

## What I work on

- Automating recurring analytical and operational processes with **Python and SQL**
- Monitoring pricing, invoices, transactions, and operational performance
- Building KPI frameworks and decision-support dashboards
- Turning complex business questions into reproducible data analyses
- Developing deeper expertise in machine learning and production-oriented data science
